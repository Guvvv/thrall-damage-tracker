# Thrall Damage Tracker

A RuneLite plugin that tracks the cumulative damage dealt by your summoned thralls and provides a lifespan countdown.

## Primarly intended for thrall only accounts tracking overall damage done for kill credit requirements.
## Features

* **Persistent Damage Tracking**: Tracks total damage dealt across multiple thrall summons. Damage only resets when the "Reset Session" button is pressed.
* **Lifespan Countdown**: Timer showing how much time your thrall has left.
* **Visual Alerts**: The timer automatically changes color (Yellow at 20s, Red at 10s) to notify you when to resummon.
* **Dual Display**: View your stats in the side panel and on-screen overlay.
## How to Use

1. Summon a thrall (Ghost, Skeleton, or Zombie).
2. The timer will start automatically upon summoning.
3. Damage will begin accumulating as soon as the thrall lands its first hit.
4. Use the **Reset Session** button in the side panel to clear the current target and damage totals.

## Configuration

* **Thrall Lifespan**: Adjust the countdown timer (60s, 90s, or 120s) to match your current Combat Achievement tier.
* **Reset on New Target**: Toggle whether damage should clear automatically when you attack a different NPC.
* **UI Customization**: Change font sizes and colors for the NPC name and damage values.
